Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about GEO and our platform.

What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

GEO is the practice of optimizing content to improve visibility and citations in AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude. Unlike traditional SEO that focuses on ranking in search result lists, GEO ensures your content is cited and referenced within AI-generated responses.

How does GEO differ from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO optimizes for ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs), while GEO optimizes for citation and reference within AI-generated content. GEO requires different strategies including conversational content, structured data, and AI-friendly formatting that traditional SEO doesn't address.

Which AI platforms do you support?

We support all major AI platforms including ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, Microsoft Copilot, and emerging AI search engines. Our Enterprise plan also includes support for custom and proprietary AI engines.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients see initial improvements in AI visibility within 2-4 weeks. Significant increases in citations and AI search presence typically occur within 6-8 weeks of implementation. Results vary based on industry competitiveness and content quality.

Can I integrate with my existing CMS?

Yes! We integrate with 50+ platforms including WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, Squarespace, Drupal, and custom CMSs. Our API allows for seamless content publishing and updates across all your digital properties.

Do you offer white-label solutions?

Our Enterprise plan includes white-label reporting and dashboard options, perfect for agencies and consultants. You can brand the platform as your own and provide GEO services to your clients.
